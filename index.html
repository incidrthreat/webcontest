How do I get selected?

The contest will run between 15 Jan and 28 Feb 2019. At the end of the contest, the Hackmethod and NetSecFocus community leaders will choose the 10 best designs based on the criteria listed below. The final 10 will be then voted on by the InfoSec community to choose the winners. Those winner's will sit in the passenger side as we have them professionally drawn and designed. So do not hesitate, submit your idea and ride along with us as we go through each design iteration to make sure your idea comes to life and ensuring your vision is the way you intended it to be.

  1. Originality/Creativity (refers to how innovative the design is)
  2. Balance and Composition (refers to the artistic arrangement of the design elements)
  3. Nerdy/Geeky relevance/message conveyed
  4. Submit your design(s) to: contest@hackmethod.com (See Rules for all details)

Rules

  1. All entries must be original creations of the contestant, have never been published, and does not contain profanity, trademarks, logos, or copyrighted works of any other person or business.

  2. The winning design will remain the property of Hackmethod LLC, and will not be returned to you. Hackmethod may use the winning design at any time after the contest. The winning designer may display their own design in a portfolio or personal collection, but may not sell or reproduce the design for any other purpose once submitted to the contest.

  3. A panel of judges representing Hackmethod will choose the top 10 submissions. The top 10 submissions will be put to a public vote to choose the winners (Date TBD).

  4. The contest begins on 15 January 2019.

  5. The entries must be received via email to contest@hackmethod.com by 11:59 p.m. (Central Standard Time) on Thursday, Feb. 28, 2019

  6. Include with entries:
    • The entry should be accompanied by your name and contact information (email initially, more to come later if chosen).
    • The entry should include details on the design or idea.
    • The entry may be any of the following art file formats if submitted electronically, anything else will be deleted: .AI (Adobe Illustrator), .JPG, .PNG or .PSD (Adobe Photoshop).

  7. Hackmethod, LLC reserves the right to terminate this contest at any time, effective on the date of issuing the termination notice, and we are under no obligation to give notice prior to the date of termination.

  8. You agree that if chosen as a contestant winner, you will sign an assignment and release form prepared by us acknowledging your acceptance and understanding of the complete contest rules including that the winning design is the property of Hackmethod, LLC.
